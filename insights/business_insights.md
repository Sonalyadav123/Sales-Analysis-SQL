## Key Findings

### 1. Top Revenue Category

Electronics generates the highest revenue.
The company should focus more on promoting electronics products.

### 2. High Value Customers

A small group of customers contributes a large portion of total revenue.
These customers can be targeted with loyalty programs.

### 3. Monthly Sales Trend

Sales increase during certain months, possibly due to:

* Festive seasons
* Promotions
* Discount campaigns

### 4. Regional Performance

Some regions have a higher average order value.
The company can increase marketing in those regions.

---

## Business Recommendations

1. Focus marketing on top-performing products.
2. Reward high-value customers with special offers.
3. Run campaigns during high-sales months.
4. Improve sales in low-performing regions.
